To our website about the lovely dog: the Australian labradoodle. An amazing dog with an intriguing history which has put the dog world

Upside down. The labradoodle is not ,as many people think, a cross from a Poodle & Labrador but a purpose bred dog created and developed with a vison: to provide an intelligent and sensitively intuitive dog of optimum health in mind and body, to be a non shedding allergy friendly companion for people who suffer from dog related allergies.
